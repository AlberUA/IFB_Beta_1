Offshore ready-made company with crypto bank and exchange license in Saint Vincent for sale. A potential investor and new owner of the company is given the opportunity to manage a fully ready-made commercial structure that performs the functions of a full-fledged cryptobank and cryptocurrency exchange. The company serves clients all over the world.

A company with crypto exchange and bank license for sale in St. Vincent

A unique opportunity to buy a ready-made company with crypto bank and exchange license in Saint Vincent is the acquisition of a company that is authorized to offer a list of the following services.

  • Opening correspondent accounts with support for cryptocurrencies in a US bank (provided that the buyer is registered).
  • Providing ready-made solutions for exchange in crypto-banking, matching and managing customer orders, cold storage, implementation of 24/7 support and insurance services.
  • Custody services for digital assets and FIAT (EUR, USD, GBP, CAD, CHF).
  • Cold and Hot Digital Asset Services: Selection of verified positions through a global auditor with full insurance provided by the world’s largest insurance organization.
  • DeFI Crypto funding and lending through a banking platform. This is an opportunity for clients to trade in physical silver and gold, in particular, to store such assets and deliver them around the world.
  • Ensuring the execution and routing of orders at the best cost: BSV, ETH, YFI, MANA, USDC, DOGE, BCH, AAVE, AG, AU, BAT, LTC, LINK, SNX, UNI, XRP, BTC, COMP, USDT, MKR, etc.
  • Issuance of Visa debit cards that are linked to the banking institution’s trading platform and customer accounts. The cards are compatible with the “Crypto to FIAT” and “FIAT to Crypto” exchange and payment mechanisms.

The company holds top certifications for encryption, custom vaults, cold storage wallet security, and from Fireblocks. The firm is also ISAE-3402 certified.
