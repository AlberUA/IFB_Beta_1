About us

At International Fintech Business, our work is based on a combination of flexibility and innovation. We are constantly transforming our approaches, introducing new services and adapting to the modern requirements of fintech and digital business. We help entrepreneurs and corporations of any scale achieve their ambitions for independence from banks.

We form a circle of trust, care and safety that unites companies, people and technology.

Company overview

International Fintech Business (IFB) is an international consulting company established in 2022 through the merger of Qiuntex and Pearly Mount Consultants. We provide comprehensive support to entrepreneurs throughout their journey, from bank licensing to the opening or acquisition of their first clients.

Benefits that make us unique

Leading experts in the banking system are at the head of IFB. We know how the regulator functions in a particular jurisdiction and which financial institution is suitable for the client's needs
We fully execute the plans of entrepreneurs and corporations for independence from banking institutions. We open corporate accounts, write compliance procedures (KYC/AML Compliance) and connect clients to the payment system (SWIFT/SEPA, VISA/MASTERCARD/UNION PAY)
We provide a ready-made and improved IT product for the payment and banking system. Our consultants help manage the end-to-end configuration process, including licensing, compliance, consulting, operational and system configuration
We conduct a preliminary assessment of financial capabilities, experience in the fintech field, and the client’s needs. We draft a roadmap and launch the process of creating, buying or selling a financial institution. As a result, we select the best option for your business
