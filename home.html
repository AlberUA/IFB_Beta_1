Complete Solutions

We can help you start a business or gain financial independence while keeping your debts. With us, you are not worried about the problems with registering a company abroad. We can also decide on the choice of jurisdiction, obtain a license to operate, implement an investment project and open a corporate account. Do you want to buy a ready-made company or create a startup from scratch? Whatever your plans, IFB is your chance to tap into the multi-billion dollar financial services market.
No modern financial organization can become competitive without introducing innovative technologies into its processes. We at IFB understand this, so we offer our clients to integrate progressive payment services into their platforms and add the ability to make transactions in cryptocurrency. The specialists of our company work closely with financial regulators, banks and other organizations, so all tasks will be performed taking into account the latest requirements and trends.
Management of a corporate ecosystem based on blockchain algorithms and smart contracts is a sure step towards optimizing the company's internal processes, increasing productivity and reducing business costs. The introduction of these technologies is not an easy task, which can be beyond the power of not only start-ups, but also large corporations. Therefore, organizations often turn to providers that provide blockchain consulting services. IFB professionals are also ready to help you with this.
If you have decided to acquire an existing financial institution, we are ready to provide you with full support: from advice and direct assistance in the implementation of the transaction to serving your first client. The IFB team has experience in assisting with the purchase of fintech projects, banks, mini-banks and further support in conducting transactions: issuing cards, integrating payment systems, opening bank accounts.
