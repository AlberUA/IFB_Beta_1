What are the benefits for businesses when acquiring ready-made companies with licenses?

Why waste time navigating through bureaucracy when you can take advantage of our streamlined approach? By purchasing a ready company with an existing license, you can focus on what truly matters - growing your business and maximizing profits.

  • Quick Start. By acquiring a company with an existing license, entrepreneurs can immediately commence providing their services or goods. This significantly reduces the time required, which is particularly crucial in a competitive environment.
  • Risk Reduction. An existing company has already undergone the process of obtaining a license and has passed necessary regulatory scrutiny. This reduces the risks associated with improper organization of processes and actions related to licensing.
  • Opportunities for Growth and Expansion. Acquiring a licensed company not only allows you to expand your business but also enables you to implement innovative ideas within an existing entity.
  • Increased Revenues. With a license in hand, your customer base will expand, and you won't miss out on lucrative deals.

With IFB you get:

  • Quick and easy purchasing process. We have well-established processes for selecting and purchasing foreign companies with open accounts. Thanks to the high expertise of our staff in providing consulting services, you won't have to wait long for the results.
  • Expert Consultations. Our lawyers and managers with experience in the banking industry will assist you in choosing the best option for acquisition and provide a thorough examination of foreign company's open accounts.
  • Reliable fintech services. We will assist you in implementing your own automated software to streamline the operations of the newly acquired enterprise and enhance your competitiveness in the foreign market.
