General provisions

This Privacy Policy sets out the rules and regulations for using the International Fintech Business (IFB) website located at ifbusiness.uk.

By accessing this website, we assume that you accept these terms. Do not continue to use the International Fintech Business (IFB) website if you do not agree to accept all of the terms and conditions set out on this page.

We collect personal information only to the extent necessary to provide you with the relevant services, and only that information that is consistent with the purposes of collecting personal data.

Our primary goals in collecting information are to enable you to use and improve our services, features and content and to enable visitors to fully experience the website.

We keep the data for as long as necessary to ensure the proper provision of our services to you.

A designated employee of the International Fintech Business (IFB) has access to the personal data of the client who works directly with him. No other specialist or third party has access to the user's confidential information without your prior consent.

Each employee of International Fintech Business (IFB) who works with personal information has signed an NDA (non-disclosure agreement) and is responsible for its dissemination.

We reserve the right to transfer your personal data to third parties only at the official request of authorized government agencies, law enforcement agencies, government officials or other third parties, when we are compelled to do so by subpoena, court order or similar legal process; or in accordance with other mandatory provisions provided by applicable law.
