  • Guaranteed Compliance. All licenses in our catalog fully comply with legislation and regulatory standards.

  • Trust from Clients and Investors. A company with not just a license but also a market history will enhance trust from clients and potential investors.

Types of licenses for cryptocurrency activities:

  • Cryptocurrency Exchange License. Allows companies to conduct the exchange of cryptocurrencies for other cryptocurrencies or fiat currencies.
  • Cryptocurrency Wallet License. Permits the provision of services for creating and managing cryptocurrency wallets used for storing and sending cryptocurrencies.
  • Cryptocurrency Payment License. Enables companies to offer services for processing payments in cryptocurrencies.
  • Cryptocurrency Issuance and Sale License. Regulates the process of issuing and selling the company's own cryptocurrencies through Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs).
  • Decentralized Finance License. Allows the provision of services related to decentralized financial products and services based on blockchain technology.
  • Cryptocurrency Arbitrage License. Permits engagement in arbitrage trading between different cryptocurrency platforms.
  • Cryptocurrency Funds and Investment License. Enables the management of investment funds focused on cryptocurrencies and blockchain.
