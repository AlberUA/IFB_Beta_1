What advantages does a business gain by acquiring an existing bank?

Acquiring a bank with a license offers several significant benefits for companies looking to enter or expand in the financial sector. Here are some key benefits:

  • Time-Saving. Starting a new bank from scratch can be a time-consuming and costly process, involving obtaining licenses, building infrastructure, and attracting clients.
  • License and Reputation. An existing bank already holds a valid license for providing banking services and has an established reputation in the market.
  • Established Customer Base. Ready banks usually have a loyal customer base, enabling quicker deposit attraction and credit provision.
  • Expansion of Services and Territory. Purchasing a bank allows you to expand your geographical presence and offer banking services in new countries.
  • Business Diversification. Acquiring an existing bank can be part of a business diversification strategy, enabling your company to enter new market sectors and offer additional financial services.

With IFB you get:

  • Quick and easy purchasing process. We have well-established processes for selecting and purchasing foreign companies with open accounts. Thanks to the high expertise of our staff in providing consulting services, you won't have to wait long for the results.
  • Expert Consultations. Our lawyers and managers with experience in the banking industry will assist you in choosing the best option for acquisition and provide a thorough examination of foreign company's open accounts.
  • Reliable fintech services. We will assist you in implementing your own automated software to streamline the operations of the newly acquired enterprise and enhance your competitiveness in the foreign market.
